## "Locations are not Villains" — a NARROW rule about Villain GROUPS, not about Villain-Deck origin (read before any Location/Villain-trigger question)

- The Revelations rulesheet's *"Locations do not count as Villains; abilities that mention Villains do not work on Locations"* means a Location is not a member of a **Villain GROUP** (Army of Evil, Hood's Gang, etc.) and isn't a fightable group-Villain. It does NOT mean "not from the Villain Deck" — Locations DO arrive via the Villain Deck, like everything else there.
- **The distinction that bit us (HYDRA Base / War Machine, 2026-06-15):** a card can be a Villain-Deck member AND a Henchman (a Villain subtype) while merely *displayed* as a Location. HYDRA Base is exactly that — a Henchman fought with Attack, sent to the Victory Pile for VP. The "Locations aren't Villains" carve-out applies to **generic Recruit-paid stronghold Locations** (Dome of Darkforce, Raft Prison, mastermind-Tactic Locations), NOT to a Henchman-that-is-a-Location.
- **Rule of thumb for future questions:** for any "does X count as a Villain / fire a defeat-a-Villain trigger" question about a Location-type card, FIRST check whether the card is also a Henchman (subtype "Henchman") or a group-Villain. If yes → it IS a Villain for triggers/defeat (the Location display is cosmetic). Only a *pure* stronghold Location (no Henchman/group identity, Recruit-paid) gets the "not a Villain" carve-out. Don't let the "Location" type tag alone settle it.
- This is, to our knowledge, the only place this conflation has bitten — but apply the check to any similar Location/Villain-trigger ambiguity.

## "Defeat a Villain" triggers (War Machine +1 Recruit, Nightbringer) vs LOCATIONS — defeating a Villain stacked ON a Location DOES count; fighting a bare *generic* Location does NOT. (HYDRA Base is a SEPARATE case — see the dedicated entry below; the old "(b) HYDRA Base = no" line here was WRONG and is superseded.)

- **THE RULING (solo, both modes):**
  - **(a) Defeat a Villain that sits ON TOP OF a Location → YES, counts as "defeating a Villain."** You defeat the Villain by the normal rule (expend Attack ≥ its Attack, put it in your Victory Pile). The Location underneath is irrelevant to that defeat — it is a separate card you have NOT touched. War Machine's "Whenever you defeat a Villain this turn, you get +1 Recruit" SHOULD fire. The observed behavior (no +1 Recruit when defeating a Villain associated with a Location) is a **BUG**, not correct rules.
  - **(b) Fight a BARE *generic* Location (a Recruit-cost Location card with no Villain on it) → NO, does NOT count as defeating a Villain.** Fighting a *generic* Location is a separate, Recruit-paid action explicitly carved out from Villain-defeat, and the rulesheet states Locations are not Villains. War Machine's +1 Recruit and Nightbringer's "defeat a Villain" SHOULD NOT fire on a generic Location fight. **NOTE: HYDRA Base is NOT a generic Recruit-paid Location — it is a Henchman with an Attack value, fought with ATTACK. See the dedicated HYDRA Base entry below; the earlier draft of this bullet that lumped HYDRA Base in with generic Locations was incorrect.**
- **CARD TEXT (inventory verbatim, docs/card-inventory/final/revelations.md):**
  - **War Machine — Military-Industrial Complex (Common B)**, line 377: SPECIAL ABILITY *"Whenever you defeat a Villain this turn, you get +1 Recruit."* — keyed on the verb "defeat a Villain."
  - **Nightbringer, The Black Light (Mandarin's Rings Henchman)**, line 728: Fight *"Reveal the top three cards of the Villain Deck. You may defeat a Villain you revealed worth 2VP or less. (Do its Fight effect.) Put the rest back in any order."* — NOTE: this is an on-FIGHT effect that lets you defeat a revealed Villain; it is not a "whenever you defeat" trigger. It targets "a Villain you revealed," so it likewise keys on Villain-status, not Location.
- **WHY (a) COUNTS — the Villain and the Location are distinct cards:**
  - Locations sit ABOVE a city space and a Villain can occupy the space below. Revelations rulesheet p.2 (verbatim): *"Once placed, Locations don't move. You can have a Villain in a city space that has a Location above it."* The Villain in the space is fought and defeated independently; defeating it is a textbook Villain defeat.
  - Core "How to Fight a Villain" (rules/Legendary_Rules-Core_Set.pdf p.13, verbatim): *"You use Attack to fight any number of Villains in the city, one at a time... Expend Attack equal to that Villain's Attack to defeat it... Put the defeated Villain and any Bystanders it had captured into your personal Victory Pile."* Defeating the Villain on a space = expend Attack, put Villain in Victory Pile. That IS "defeating a Villain"; War Machine's trigger conditions on exactly this event. The Location underneath is untouched by the act.
- **WHY (b) DOES NOT COUNT (generic Recruit-paid Locations) — fighting a Location is a separate, Recruit-paid action and Locations aren't Villains:**
  - Revelations rulesheet p.2 "Locations" (verbatim): *"You can fight a Location by spending the listed amount of [Recruit], putting it into your Victory Pile, and doing any Fight ability the Location may have."* You pay **Recruit**, not **Attack** — a categorically different action from Core p.13 Villain defeat (which expends Attack). The rulesheet uses "fight a Location," never "defeat a Villain," for this. This is the rule for Locations that have a printed RECRUIT cost.
  - Revelations rulesheet p.2 "Location Clarifications" (verbatim): *"Locations do not count as Villains. Special abilities that mention Villains do not work on Locations."* War Machine's "defeat a **Villain**" and Nightbringer's "defeat a **Villain** you revealed" are special abilities that mention Villains → for a card that is purely a Location (paid in Recruit, no Attack value), they DO NOT work.
- **DIGITAL-BUILD CAVEAT (why the (a) bug likely happens):** This project models several Revelations Location/Villain interactions by reusing the Location `fightEffect` plumbing. If a Villain that enters a space WITH a Location above it is being routed through Location-fight code (Recruit-paid path) instead of the normal Villain-defeat path, the "defeat a Villain" trigger hook won't fire — matching the user's observation. The rules-correct fix: defeating the VILLAIN on the space must go through the normal Attack-based Villain-defeat path and fire all "whenever you defeat a Villain" triggers; only the act of fighting a generic Recruit-paid LOCATION itself is exempt. Do NOT suppress the Villain-defeat trigger just because a Location shares the space.
- **SOLO HANDLING (Golden Solo / What If?):** No divergence. Mode-agnostic; neither solo ruleset alters "defeat a Villain", the Location fight action, or "Locations do not count as Villains".
- **SOURCE:** War Machine + Nightbringer text — inventory lines 377, 728 (verbatim). Location fight action + "do not count as Villains" — Revelations rulesheet p.2 (verbatim). Villain defeat = expend Attack + put in Victory Pile — Core p.13 (verbatim). Villain-can-sit-on-a-Location — Revelations rulesheet p.2 (verbatim).
- **CONFIDENCE:** (a) defeating a Villain on a Location counts → War Machine +1 Recruit SHOULD fire = **SETTLED**. (b) generic Recruit-paid bare Location fight does NOT count = **SETTLED**. HYDRA Base is the exception — see dedicated entry.
