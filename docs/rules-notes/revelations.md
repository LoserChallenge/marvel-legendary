## "Locations are not Villains" — a NARROW rule about Villain GROUPS, not about Villain-Deck origin (read before any Location/Villain-trigger question)

- The Revelations rulesheet's *"Locations do not count as Villains; abilities that mention Villains do not work on Locations"* means a Location is not a member of a **Villain GROUP** (Army of Evil, Hood's Gang, etc.) and isn't a fightable group-Villain. It does NOT mean "not from the Villain Deck" — Locations DO arrive via the Villain Deck, like everything else there.
- **The distinction that bit us (HYDRA Base / War Machine, 2026-06-15):** a card can be a Villain-Deck member AND a Henchman (a Villain subtype) while merely *displayed* as a Location. HYDRA Base is exactly that — a Henchman fought with Attack, sent to the Victory Pile for VP. The "Locations aren't Villains" carve-out applies to a **pure stronghold Location** (Carnival of Wonders, Laser Maze, Dome of Darkforce, Raft Prison, mastermind-Tactic Locations) — a card whose ONLY card-type identity is "Location", NOT to a Henchman-that-is-a-Location. The line is CARD-TYPE IDENTITY (does the card also carry the Henchman/Villain subtype?), NOT the payment method (Recruit vs Attack) — see the corrected entries below.
- **Rule of thumb for future questions:** for any "does X count as a Villain / fire a defeat-a-Villain trigger" question about a Location-type card, FIRST check whether the card is also a Henchman (subtype "Henchman") or a group-Villain. If yes → it IS a Villain for triggers/defeat (the Location display is cosmetic). Only a *pure* Location (its sole card-type is Location, no Henchman/group-Villain subtype) gets the "not a Villain" carve-out. Don't let the "Location" type tag alone settle it; and do NOT use payment method (Recruit vs Attack) as the discriminator.
- This is, to our knowledge, the only place this conflation has bitten — but apply the check to any similar Location/Villain-trigger ambiguity.
