## New Keywords

- **X-Gene**: "X-Gene [CLASS]: [Effect]" means "If you have a [CLASS] card in your discard pile, [Effect]." You can use a card's X-Gene ability only once per card played, no matter how many matching cards are in your discard pile. Cards played this turn are in front of you (not in your discard pile) until end of turn.
- **Piercing Energy**: A new kind of points. You can fight a Villain or Mastermind by spending Piercing points equal to that enemy's printed VP value, ignoring Attack, Attack modifiers, and special conditions (automatically rescuing Human Shields). Cannot target cards with no printed VP (e.g., Shadow-X Villains, Master Strike token Villains).
- **Berserk**: "Berserk" means "Discard the top card of your deck. You get +Attack equal to the discarded card's printed Attack." (e.g., "2+ Attack" counts as 2.) "Berserk, Berserk, Berserk" = discard three cards. Gives no benefit from Recruit or Piercing values.
- **Soaring Flight**: "When you recruit this Hero, set it aside. At the end of this turn, add it to your new hand as an extra card."
- **Lightshow**: Some Heroes have "Lightshow: [Effect]." Once per turn, if you played at least two Lightshow cards this turn, you can use a single Lightshow ability from any of those cards. Three or more Lightshow cards still use only one ability.
- **Dominate**: "Dominate" means "Put those Heroes under this enemy. This enemy gets +1 Attack for each Hero it's Dominating." When you fight that enemy, put one Dominated Hero into each player's discard pile (you choose who gets which). KO any excess. If a Villain escapes, Dominated Heroes go to the Escape Pile too.
- **Human Shields**: "This Villain captures N Human Shields" = captures top N cards of Bystander Stack face-down. You can't fight a Villain with Human Shields. Pay Attack equal to that Villain's Attack value to rescue one Human Shield at random. Human Shields still count as Bystanders. Attack values marked with `*` indicate Human Shield carriers.
- **Traps**: A new card type in the Villain Deck. When played, presents a challenge to complete this turn. Success = Trap goes to Victory Pile for its VP. Failure = KO the Trap and suffer consequences (after drawing new hand at end of turn). Traps are NOT Villains and don't enter the city (unless failure effect says otherwise).
- **Divided**: Hero cards split into two halves, each with its own class, ability, and base value. When played, choose one half to use.
- **Ascend**: Some Villains and Horrors "ascend" to become new Masterminds mid-game. The ascending card transforms into a Mastermind with its own Master Strike ability. (Used by Mastermind Jason Wyngarde, Lady Mastermind, Shadow of the Disciple Horror.)
- **Teleport**: "Instead of playing this card, you may set it aside. At the end of this turn, add it to your new hand as an extra card."
- **Token Cards**: Optional physical cards representing summoned Villains or Masterminds generated by other card effects. Identified by a Token icon in the upper right. 9 tokens total in this expansion.

### Implementation Notes

- **Dark Phoenix special win condition**: "Dark Phoenix Wins: When the Hero Deck is empty" — functions like an Evil Wins condition, checked continuously. This is specific to Dark Phoenix, not a general rule.
- **Ascending Villains**: Mastermind (Jason Wyngarde) and Lady Mastermind both ascend to become new Masterminds on Ambush. Shadow of the Disciple Horror also ascends. These create additional Masterminds mid-game with their own Master Strike abilities but no Tactics deck.
- **Deathbird tactics**: All 4 become Villains in the city after defeat, each with unique Escape abilities. Shi'ar Elite Bodyguards can recycle back into Deathbird's Tactics.
- **Fight to the End** (Horror): Changes win condition from 4 Tactic defeats to 5 total fights (4 Tactics + 1 Mastermind). Significant for Golden Solo's Final Showdown logic.
- **Horror stacking**: Multiple Mastermind Attack Horrors stack (Enraged +2, Maniacal +1, Tyrant +3, Growing Threat +1 per tactic).
- **Chain draws**: Several Horrors and cards trigger additional Villain Deck draws that can cascade.
