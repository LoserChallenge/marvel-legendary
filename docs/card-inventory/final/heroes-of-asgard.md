## New Keywords

- **Worthy**: You are Worthy if you have a Hero that costs 5 or more. "Your Heroes" and "Heroes you have" include cards in your hand, cards you played this turn, and Hero Artifacts you control. Heroes in your deck and discard pile don't count.
- **Thrown Artifact**: Put this card on the bottom of your deck and use its ability. You can throw as many Artifacts as you want in a turn. You can only throw during your turn.
- **Villainous Weapon**: A new card type (not a Villain). When played from the Villain Deck, the Weapon is captured by the Villain in the city closest to the Villain Deck. If no Villains in the city, KO the Weapon. Adds its printed Attack bonus to the holding Villain/Mastermind. When you defeat a Villain/Mastermind holding Weapons, you gain those Weapons as Artifacts. As Artifacts in your control, they have 0 cost, no color/class, and don't count as Hero or Villain cards. You never get the printed Attack bonus — only the specific Artifact abilities.
- **Conqueror**: "[Space] Conqueror N" means this card gets +N Attack while any Villain is in [Space]. The card itself does not need to be in that space.
- **Artifact**: A card that stays in play in front of you. Use "once per turn" abilities each turn you control it. You only "played" an Artifact on the turn you put it out (for Superpower triggers). Artifacts you control count as "your Heroes" for reveal effects.

**Royal Decree** (Rare)

- SUPERPOWER: [HEROES OF ASGARD]: Each player who is Worthy draws a card. Each Villain that isn't worth at least 5VP gets -1 Attack this turn.

### Schemes

---

**Asgardian Test of Worth**
Setup: 11 Twists.
Twist 1-7: Each player who is not Worthy discards a card. Then, if at least half the players (round up) are not Worthy, put this Twist next to the Scheme as a "Moral Failing."
Twist 8-11: Put this Twist next to the Scheme as a "Moral Failing."
Evil Wins: When there are 5 Moral Failings.

---

**The Dark World of Svartalfheim**
Setup: 10 Twists.
Twist: Put this Twist next to a city space or HQ space that doesn't already have one, as "Eternal Darkness."
Special Rules: Villains in city spaces with Eternal Darkness get +1 Attack. To recruit a Hero in an HQ space with Eternal Darkness, you must pay an extra 1 Recruit.
Evil Wins: When all city spaces or all HQ spaces are covered in Eternal Darkness.

---

**Ragnarok, Twilight of the Gods**
Setup: 11 Twists.
Twist: Choose a Villain from your Victory Pile worth at least 2VP to enter the city. Then, if the total Attack of Villains in the city is at least as high as the Guardian Attack listed below, put this Twist next to the Scheme as a "Guardian Defeated."

| Twist | Guardian | Attack Threshold |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Balder | 11 |
| 2 | Odin | 24 |
| 3 | Vidar | 19 |
| 4 | Tyr | 16 |
| 5 | Heimdall | 12 |
| 6 | Frey | 7 |
| 7 | Frigga | 8 |
| 8-11 | Warriors of Valhalla | 6 |

Evil Wins: When there are 5 Guardians Defeated.

---

**War of the Frost Giants**
Setup: 9 Twists.

Twist 8-9: Same effect, then a Frost Giant Invader from each player's Victory Pile enters the city.
Evil Wins: When there are 5 Frost Giant Invaders in the city and/or Escape Pile.

## Notes

### Card Misprints

- **Laufey Ambush text** — the printed card says "Casket of Eternal Winters" in the Ambush text, but "Casket of Ancient Winters" in the Escape text. The weapon card is named "The Casket of Ancient Winters." This is a confirmed misprint on the card itself — the Ambush should reference "Ancient Winters." Effect text above preserves the card's exact wording.

### Solo Mode Notes (for future implementation)

- **The Mangog** — "player on your right" has no valid target in solo.
- **Naglfar, Longship of Fingernails** — same "player on your right" issue.
- **Asgardian Test of Worth** — "at least half the players (round up)" = in solo, you must be Worthy to avoid Moral Failings on Twists 1-7.
