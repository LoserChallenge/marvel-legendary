## New Keywords

- **Undercover**: "Put that Hero into your Victory Pile. It's worth 1VP." Used to thin your deck by removing S.H.I.E.L.D. cards (Agents, Troopers, Officers, or even main heroes). If you play a card and it sends itself Undercover, you still get its Recruit and Attack. Sending S.H.I.E.L.D. Heroes Undercover increases your S.H.I.E.L.D. Level.
- **S.H.I.E.L.D. Level**: "Your S.H.I.E.L.D. Level is the number of S.H.I.E.L.D. and/or HYDRA cards in your Victory Pile." Cards say "S.H.I.E.L.D. Level N: [effect]" — use the ability only if your level is N or higher. Counts any card with S.H.I.E.L.D. or HYDRA team icons, or "S.H.I.E.L.D." or "Hydra" in card name, Villain Group name, or Mastermind name. Never consumes the cards — just checks.
- **Hydra Level**: "The Hydra Level is the number of S.H.I.E.L.D. and/or HYDRA cards in the Escape Pile." Same counting rules as S.H.I.E.L.D. Level but in the Escape Pile. Putting cards from the S.H.I.E.L.D. Officer Stack into the Escape Pile increases it, but this is NOT a Villain "escape" — does not KO a Hero from the HQ.
- **Adapting Masterminds**: No lead card — just 4 Mastermind Tactic cards. Whichever Tactic is currently on top acts as the Mastermind (use only its rules, ignore the rest). "Adapt" = shuffle the Tactics and randomly put one on top, face up. When you fight, put the top Tactic into your Victory Pile, do its Fight effect, then Adapt.

**S.H.I.E.L.D. vs. HYDRA War**

**Hail Hydra**
Setup: 11 Twists.

**HYDRA Helicarriers Hunt Heroes**
Setup: 8 Twists. Add an extra Hero.
Twist: Stack this Twist next to the Scheme. Then for each Twist stacked there, choose a different Hero Class ([STRENGTH], [INSTINCT], [COVERT], [TECH], [RANGE]), to a maximum of 5. KO each Hero from the HQ that has any of those Hero Classes.
Evil Wins: When there are 18 non-grey Heroes in the KO pile.

---

**Secret Empire of Betrayal**
Setup: 11 Twists. Randomly pick 5 cards that cost 5 or less from an additional Hero. Shuffle them to form a "Dark Loyalty" deck.
Twist: Shuffle this Twist into the Dark Loyalty deck as a "Vicious Betrayal." Then reveal the top card of that deck. If it's a Hero, gain it. If it's a Vicious Betrayal, put it next to the Scheme and each other player gains a Wound.
Evil Wins: When there are 6 Vicious Betrayals next to the Scheme.
